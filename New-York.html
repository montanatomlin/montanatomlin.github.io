Weekend in New York City

Friday Afternoon

Sightseeing and Dinner

Start off your weekend with everyone’s number one spot on their NYC itinerary – Times Square. Huge billboards and flashing lights await you as you’re welcomed by notorious sights and people.Head to Dinner at TacoVision. Escape to the Margarita Garden at Taco Vision, a fun taco bar and mezcaleria that has over 200 tequilas & mezcals.

Friday Evening

Friday Night Out at the Delancey

The DL is a multi‐level lounge, restaurant and event space located in Manhattan's Lower East Side.Guests can enjoy dinner on the first floor with a great atmosphere lit by chandeliers dripping in crystals or head straight upstairs to our rooftop lounge. You can enjoy spectacular views of the Williamsburg Bridge on the third floor which is the rooftop.

Saturday Morning

Brunch at Poco

Poco Restaurant & Bar is a Spanish inspired Tapas restaurant by night and a Boozy Brunch spot by day

Saturday Afternoon

Shopping and Sightseeing

First visit the High Line.High Line is a public park built on a historic freight rail line. The park has a lively calendar of events, which includes walking tours, spoken word nights, tai chi sessions, and art installations. Second, SoHo is the perfect NYC shopping neighborhood—it has a unique mix of high-end designer stores and mid to low-end shops that appeal to every type of shopper. Lastly, head to Harlem to see the Apollo Theater.The world-famous Apollo Theater has been a staple in the Harlem community since 1934 and has featured musical giants for generations.

Saturday Evening

Dinner at Angel of Harlem and Night Out at Westlight

Angel of Harlem serve Contemporary American cuisine with Latin & Caribbean flavors. The food and cocktail menus offer authentic specialties prepared with local, fresh ingredients. The restaurant also serves as a platform for local artists—painters, musicians, DJs, designers and poets—to share their talent.Westlight is a 22nd-floor rooftop bar featuring views of New York City from atop The William Vale in Williamsburg.

Sunday Morning

Brunch at BLVD Bistro

BLVD Bistro is a neighborhood restaurant in an historic brownstone with a cozy dining room and a dynamic Southern-inspired menu.

Sunday Afternoon

Visit the Empire State Building

Together with the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building is probably the most iconic American building. Visiting the Empire State Building is a must if you're in NYC.
